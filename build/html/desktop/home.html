How it Works

1

Airlines donate Wifi

A majority of airline companies offer wireless internet access on their routes, but are often costly. Our idea is that if a traveler promise to work on a project for an organisation during the travel, airlines that sponsor this project, will offer them wifi access for free.

2

Organisations upload projects

Let's say the organisation Save The Coral Reefs needs to add a feature on their homepage where they can post blog posts but lacks the knowledge to implement it. What they could do is to signup for a TimeBird organisations account, upload a short description of the project with optional photos and videos.

3

People donate time to help out

A week before the donor is traveling, he signup for a Donor TimeBird account, he search for a project that fit his skill-set and find the "Save The Coral Reefs" project. He applies to that project
